Odesa Theater of Opera and Ballet

The Odesa National Academic Opera and Ballet Theater is one of the five most beautiful theaters in the world.
This is the first opera house built in Ukraine.
Forbes magazine included the Odesa Opera among the most attractive attractions in Eastern Europe.
